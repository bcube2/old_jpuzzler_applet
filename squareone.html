JPuzzler: Square One 
JPuzzler Instructions:
>
 Use Right-Mouse-Drag to rotate the puzzle in space.
>
 Use Left-Mouse-Drag to perform moves.  Drag a specific face to its destination to perform the move.
>
 Use 'M' to Mix the puzzle, 'R' to Reset it, and 'B' to take back a move.
>
 Check out the Puzzler and JPuzzler homepages!
